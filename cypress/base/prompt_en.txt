Non-existence assertions
You can verify that elements do not exist to generate not.exist assertions, but only when you provide an exact CSS selector or when you use text-based targeting. Natural language descriptions for non-existence are not currently supported. Additionally, ensure that your intention is to verify that the element does not exist in the DOM, not that it is not visible.

'verify #error-message does not exist'
'the ".loading-spinner" should not exist'
'make sure the "Error" message does not exist'
'confirm "Reset" button does not exist'
